# Revenue-Performance-Analysis-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing sales and revenue performance with key KPIs, insights, and trends to support data driven decisions.


# Project Overview
This project provides an analytical Power BI dashboard designed to evaluate revenue performance across sales representatives, product categories, customer segments, regions, and yearly trends. The dashboard visualizes key business metrics and highlights insights that support strategic decision making.


# Project Objectives
* Evaluate overall revenue and sales performance.
* Identify trends and changes across years and segments.
* Analyze revenue distribution across teams, categories, and regions.
* Highlight insights that help optimize the sales funnel and improve conversions.


# Dataset Description
* Contains sales and revenue records across multiple dimensions.
* Includes fields such as: Revenue, Sales Rep, Product Category, Team, Region, Customer Segment, Order ID, Stage, and Year.
* Structured to support dimensional analysis and KPI calculations.

  
# Tools
* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Excel


# Insights
* Revenue peaked in 2023 but declined noticeably in 2024 despite stable performance in most sales dimensions.
* Customer Segment performance shows SME and Retail leading, with Corporate slightly lower in average revenue.


# Data Storytelling Framework
* The company has shown stable performance across sales reps, product categories, customer segments, and regions over recent years.
* Despite this stability, annual revenue in 2024 declined significantly after reaching its highest point in 2023, without any major drops in other performance indicators.
* Why did revenue decrease in 2024 despite stable metrics across the sales structure? What part of the funnel or market shift caused this drop?.
* The decline is likely due to changes in customer conversion behavior rather than internal sales performance. Since deal size, category performance, and regional distribution remained stable, the issue appears to be within the sales funnel, particularly in converting opportunities into closed deals.
